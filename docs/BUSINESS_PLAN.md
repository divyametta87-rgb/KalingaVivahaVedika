# Business & Go-To-Market Plan

This document outlines the operational, legal, and financial planning for launching **Kalinga Vivaha Vedika** as a real-world platform.

## 1. Domain Registration & Branding
- **Domain Name Considerations**: `kalingavivahavedika.com`, `kalingamatrimony.in`.
- **Registrar:** Namecheap, Google Domains, or GoDaddy (Look for initial year discounts, usually ~$10-15/year).
- **Email:** Set up professional email routing (e.g., `support@kalingavivahavedika.com`) using Zoho Mail (free tier) or Google Workspace.

## 2. Legal Pre-requisites
*Before accepting real user data or payments, the following legal documents must be drafted and hosted on the site:*
- **Privacy Policy:** Must explicitly state how sensitive data (DOB, caste, photos, phone numbers) is stored, processed, and deleted upon request.
- **Terms & Conditions:** Disclaimers protecting the platform from liability regarding user behavior, fake profiles, and failed matches.
- **Refund Policy:** Crucial for payment gateway approval. Must state conditions for refunding premium subscriptions.
- **Data Deletion Flow:** Users must have an automated way to delete their profile entirely to comply with data protection laws.

## 3. Cost & Pricing Models (Monetization)
The platform will launch with a "Freemium" model.

### Free Tier (Core User Acquisition)
- Create a detailed profile.
- Upload up to 3 photos.
- Browse matches but contact details/chat are locked.
- Send up to 2 "Interests" per day.

### Premium Subscription (Paid)
- **Pricing:** e.g., ₹999/month or ₹2499/3 months.
- Unlock unlimited chat and contact number views for mutual matches.
- See who viewed your profile.
- Priority listing in search results.
- **Payment Gateway:** Razorpay or PayU (Ideal for Indian markets, requires business registration/KYC).

## 4. Initial Marketing & Seeding
- **Goal:** Reach 1,000 profiles quickly. A matchmaking app without matches is dead.
- **Strategy:** Free premium memberships for the first 500 verified signups. Reach out to community groups, Facebook clusters, and WhatsApp groups for initial seeding.
